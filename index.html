Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move the tetrominoes to your desired location. To rotate, press the UP arrow key. Press the DOWN arrow key continuously, to drop the tetromino faster. Your goal is to create a filled row with the shapes. Once a row is complete, it will disappear and 10 points will be added to your score. If the tetrominoes reach the top of the grid, its GAME OVER! Good luck!
